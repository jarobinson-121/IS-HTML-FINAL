About Me

Who I Am.

Above you can see my family, the most important people in my life. I have three brothers, a sister, and two loving parents, each of whom I couldn't fathom living without, even in the hard times.

On the left you can see me in one of my favorite roles: musician. Music plays a major role in my life, as it has in many of yours. I love playing music, as a cellist with some training with the guitar and ukulele, or just listening to good music.

On the right you see me just before my mission, one of the shortest, yet most important journeys of my life. I loved being in Germany and hope to return.
